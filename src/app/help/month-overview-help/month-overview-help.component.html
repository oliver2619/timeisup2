Month overview help

Overtime

This field shows the accumulated hours of overtime for this month. Days in the future are not taken into account for this calculation.

View day

By pressing this button you navigate to the respective day overview.

Absences

By pressing this button you mark the specified day as a full day of absence. The degree of absence can be edited later or removed again.

Edit day

By pressing this button you can edit the respective day.

Delete day

By pressing this button next to a listed day you delete the entire recordings for the respective working day. Any degree of absence will also be reset.

Delete entire month

By pressing this button in the menu bar you delete the entire month. You will be asked, wether the calculated month's hours of overtime will be saved or not.
